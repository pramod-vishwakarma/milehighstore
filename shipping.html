Shipping & Delivery

We offer a number of different shipping and delivery options. Cost and cut off times may vary depending on your delivery destination & store location of purchase, so please check the specific requirements.

The approximate time frame of delivery will be mentioned in the order confirmation.

You can track the status of your package with the tracking number provided by the courier partner.

The delivery partner will attempt delivery, although if the order can't be received at the first attempt the further attempts will be made under the policy of the courier partner.

If the package appears to be damaged or not the exact product, please don't take the delivery. Kindly get in touch with our support team.

There may be additional charges applicable for gift-wrapping items.

For being able to sign for the delivery acceptance, the receiver has to be at least 18 years old.

As the delivery of a product can be delayed or obstructed due to unforeseen circumstances, Mile High store cannot be held responsible for any such circumstantial delay.
